Tomato Curry Recipe

This dish combines tomatoes, onions and spices to create an aromatic and flavorful experience. It's one of my go-to recipes and is great when paired with rice or pasta. It can also be used as a sauce with boiled eggs or fish. Once the ingredients are prepared, it only takes about 20 minutes to cook!

Ingredients:

These are the ingredients I like to use. Feel free to modify the quantities according to your tastes. You also don't need everything on this list to try it out. As long as you have tomatoes, onions, oil and a few spices, it will still turn out great. (Though I highly recommend coming back to this recipe when you do have all these ingredients!)

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Cumin
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Turmeric
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Curry Leaves
  • Green Chili Peppers

Instructions:

  1. Start heating some oil in a pot and pour some cumin in it.
  2. Cut up a large onion into roughly 2cm square pieces. Wait until the cumin starts to sizzle and then add the chopped onion into the pot. Stir occasionally.
